About this app

  • Instructions for using the app
    • WASD keys and stanard arrow keys will work for navigation
      • Both WASD and arrow keys were implented to account for people who have different dominant hands.
    • W or up arrow to move up
    • A or left arrow to move previous
    • S or down arrow to move down
    • D or right arrow to move next
    • Enter key on keyboard or numpad will select
  • Test results
